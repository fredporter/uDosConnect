# Family Wiki

`/wiki` is the beginner-friendly learning lane for `uDOS-docs`.

Use it for:

- short family introductions
- quick practical how-tos
- learning units and checklists
- simple cross-repo orientation

Use `/docs` for deeper public reference.

Use `docs/knowledge/` for the **General Knowledge Library** (survival-guide
articles, depth and research standards, exemplar projects); see
`docs/general-knowledge-library.md`.
